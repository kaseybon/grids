Thoughts

Out of the box the grid system was easy to setup and use... until I realized it wasn't responsive out of the box. You need to include a second stylesheet to make it squishy. Then you need to use a whole new set of classes that correspond with preset break points. If you forget what their breakpoints are this can get confusing. Then if you need a new breakpoint you have to create your own custom grid which I didn't really dive into.

Once you figure out how the classes work together it is easy to use but it just seems overly complicated. Even though it is complicated and has a ton of classes it is nice to have complete control over how many columns display at certain break points. If you want to jump from 4 columns down to 2 columns you can do that. This also lets you "span multiple columns" easily.
